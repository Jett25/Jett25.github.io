Satellites

Satellites give information about Earth's clouds, oceans, land and air. Satellites can also observe wildfires, volcanoes and smoke. It helps scientists predict weather and climate. It helps farmers know what crops to plant. Satellites take pictures that help scientists learn about Earth, the solar system and the universe. There are also internet satellites.

Spacecraft's

A spacecraft is a vehicle that is designed to fly in outer space. Spacecraft's are used for a variety of purposes, including communications, earth observation, meteorology, navigation, space colonization, planetary exploration, and transportation of humans and cargo.

Orbital Launch Vehicles

Orbital launch vehicles are used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond
